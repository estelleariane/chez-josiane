About

Sewing has always been a passion and as far as i can remember, it all started as a hobby. I was always attracted by fashion and will use all opportunity i have to add my personnal touch to almost every piece of garment i can put my hand on. i started by styling my dolls and before i knew it i was making clothes for my frineds and family. Before long, through word of mouth i was having actual customers. That is when i decided to take some classes and turn my passion into business.
